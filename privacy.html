SimplyDose Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2026  |  Last Updated: May 9, 2026

Introduction

We created SimplyDose to help people taking GLP-1 medications stay organized, track their progress, and feel more in control of their health journey.

Using SimplyDose involves entrusting us with certain information (and, in some cases, a small number of carefully selected service providers that help us run the app). We treat that responsibility with care.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect
  • How we use it
  • Who we share it with
  • Your rights and choices
  • How to contact us

We have also included a dedicated Health Data section below with additional details about how we handle sensitive health information.

Where This Policy Applies

This Privacy Policy applies to the SimplyDose iOS application. SimplyDose is currently available only as an iOS app on the United States Apple App Store. We do not currently offer an Android app, and we do not operate a marketing website beyond the page where this Privacy Policy is hosted.

PlatformHow it works
iOS AppRequires creating an account using a magic link sent to your email address (managed by Supabase). Your health entries and account data are stored in our Supabase database, associated with your account.

Key Terms (Plain English)

  • Health data: Information about your health that you enter into the app (for example, GLP-1 injections, side effects, weight, and any notes you write).
  • Account information: The email address you use to sign in with a magic link, plus an internal user ID our system creates for your account.
  • Service providers: Trusted companies we work with to help operate the app. These providers handle data only on our behalf and under contract.

What We Collect (and Why)

Account Information

  • Email address (used for magic-link sign-in)
  • An internal user ID associated with your account

Why? So you can sign in, save your data, and access your information across sessions.

Health Data (Optional)

If you choose to track your health in SimplyDose, we process the information you enter, such as:

  • GLP-1 injection records (date, site, dose, time)
  • Side effects and wellness notes
  • Weight and daily check-ins
  • Any other tracking information you choose to log

Why? To provide SimplyDose's core features and help you stay organized.

Free-Text Notes

Some areas of the app let you type free-text notes. Because you control what you type, please avoid entering information that personally identifies you or others in these fields.

Legal Bases for Processing

SimplyDose is offered only in the United States. We process your information to provide the app you signed up for, to keep it secure and reliable, to comply with applicable laws, and to respond to lawful requests from regulators, courts, or law enforcement when required.

Device & Technical Information

We (and our service providers) may automatically collect limited technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash reports, and basic diagnostics — used to keep the app reliable and working properly.

Payment and Subscription Information

SimplyDose is currently available as a free app. If and when we introduce paid subscriptions, all payments will be processed by the Apple App Store. We will not receive your full payment card details. We will update this policy before launching paid subscriptions.

Analytics & Attribution

SimplyDose does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising, or attribution tools. We do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not send your health entries to any analytics service. All data transmitted by the app is encrypted in transit.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, export, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain uses of your data. You can manage many choices directly in the app's Settings. You can also email us at SimplyDoseapp@gmail.com.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, the right to correct it, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. SimplyDose does not sell your personal information and does not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Who We Share Data With (and Why)

We share data only with service providers that help us run SimplyDose.

VendorPurpose
SupabaseDatabase and authentication for account information and health entries
Apple (App Store / App Store Connect)App distribution, crash and performance reporting

We do not sell your data. We do not share your health data for targeted advertising.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information, including access controls and encryption in transit. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work hard to protect your information.

Data Controller

SimplyDose LLC (Missouri) is the entity responsible for your information under this Privacy Policy. For privacy requests or questions, contact: SimplyDoseapp@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

SimplyDose is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected information from someone under 18, we will delete it.

Important: SimplyDose Is Not a Medical Device

SimplyDose is a tracking and organization tool. It is not a medical device, it does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and it is not intended to be used as a substitute for professional medical advice. Always talk to your healthcare provider about your medications, dosing, and any health concerns. Do not rely on SimplyDose to make medical decisions.

In an emergency, call 911 or your local emergency services. Do not use the app to communicate medical emergencies.

Exporting or Deleting Your Data

You can delete your account and associated health entries directly from the app's Settings screen. You can also email us at SimplyDoseapp@gmail.com if you need help.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted at simplydose.github.io/privacy. If changes are material, we will provide additional notice where required.

Contact Us

Email us anytime at SimplyDoseapp@gmail.com

SimplyDose Health Data Privacy Section

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

This section supplements our main Privacy Policy and explains how we handle sensitive health information.

What Counts as Health Data in SimplyDose

  • GLP-1 injections (date, site, time, dosage)
  • Side effects and well-being notes
  • Weight and daily check-ins
  • Any related wellness or tracking information you choose to store

Health Data Storage

We store health data securely in Supabase, separately structured from any non-sensitive analytics or technical information. Your health entries are stored in our Supabase database and tied to your account and an internal user ID.

Sources of Health Data

We collect health data only from you, when you enter information in the app. SimplyDose does not currently import data from Apple Health or any other source.

Why We Use Health Data

We use health data to provide SimplyDose's core functionality, help you track your progress, improve app reliability and security, and comply with legal obligations. We do not use health data for targeted advertising. We do not sell health data.

How We Share Health Data

We share health data only in limited circumstances: with service providers necessary to operate the app, for support if you contact us, for legal requirements, and in corporate transactions with notice where required. We do not share health data for targeted advertising.

Your Rights Regarding Health Data

You have the right to access, export, delete, and withdraw consent for your health data. Most actions can be done directly in the app's Settings. Contact: SimplyDoseapp@gmail.com

Important Reminder

SimplyDose is a tracking tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider about medications, dosing, side effects, and any health concerns.
