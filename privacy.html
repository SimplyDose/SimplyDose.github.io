SimplyDose Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

What We Collect

SimplyDose collects the following information to provide app functionality:

  • Email address (for account creation and sign-in)
  • Medication name, dose, and injection schedule
  • Injection site, pain level, and shot notes
  • Body weight logs
  • Symptom logs and side effect tracking
  • Height, date of birth, and goal weight (optional)
  • App usage data

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored securely on Supabase servers located in the United States. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We do not store your data on your device beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide SimplyDose app functionality — tracking your GLP-1 medication doses, progress, and symptoms. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

Health Data Disclaimer

SimplyDose is not a medical device and is not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. All information in the app is for personal tracking purposes only. Always follow your prescriber's guidance.

Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, go to Profile → Reset and Start Over, or contact us at joshuamunger@outlook.com and we will delete your data within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

SimplyDose is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes via email.
